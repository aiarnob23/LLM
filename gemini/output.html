The next total solar eclipse visible in the contiguous United States will occur on August 23, 2044. This eclipse will be visible at sunset in parts of Montana, North Dakota, and South Dakota, after traveling through Greenland and Canada. Another total solar eclipse is predicted for March 30, 2033, which will pass over Alaska. Following the 2044 event, the next total solar eclipse with a path covering more than just a few states in the U.S. will be on August 12, 2045.
